Humidity

This figure addresses absolute humidity as it relates to latitude. It is important to remember that absoulte humidity is the measure of water vapor in the air, regardless of the air temperature. Relative humidity incorporates air temperature - which makes an area "feel" humid. So while cities like New Orealeans, LA and Houston, TX have the highest relative humidity, Seattle, WA has one of the highest levels of absolute humidity in the U.S. - even though it does not "feel" humid.

This figure shows the absolute humidity generally increases with latitude - analysis shows a weak, positive relationship. However, as this is absolute measures, not relatives measure, there is no strong correlation.
